Consular Affairs

The Embassy of Lebanon in Islamabad is committed to providing efficient and professional services to Lebanese nationals and the public in Pakistan.

Passport Services

  • Renewal: Processing for Biometric and 2003-model passports.
  • Emergency Travel: Issuance of Laissez-Passer for citizens with lost or expired passports.
  • Loss Report: Procedures for reporting and replacing lost Lebanese travel documents.

Civil Status Registration

  • Birth: Registration of children born in Pakistan to ensure Lebanese citizenship rights.
  • Marriage/Divorce: Official documentation of family status changes.
  • Death: Necessary procedures for the repatriation of remains or local documentation.

Attestation & Power of Attorney

  • Wakala: Drafting General and Special Powers of Attorney for use in Lebanon.
  • Commercial: Legalization of invoices, certificates of origin, and trade documents.
  • Personal: Attestation of educational degrees and medical certificates.
